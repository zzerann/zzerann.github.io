Hello, I'm Zeran Su (苏泽然)

I am an RL engineer at the National Embodied Intelligence Robotics Innovation Center (Humanoid Innovation Department), working closely with Jony Zhang. I received my M.Sc. in Human and Biological Robotics from Imperial College London (2023), and my B.A.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Robotics & Automation from the University of Toronto (2022). Previously, I was a research intern at Tencent Robotics X. My research interests lie in humanoid whole-body control, reinforcement learning for locomotion, motion retargeting, and Sim2Real deployment.
